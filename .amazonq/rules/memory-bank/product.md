# Product Overview

## Project Purpose
FilamentLabelizer is an AI-powered 3D printing filament management application that automates the process of scanning, analyzing, and labeling filament spools. The app uses computer vision and AI to extract filament information from spool images and generates professional labels for organization and inventory management.

## Key Features
- **AI-Powered Spool Recognition**: Uses Google Gemini AI to analyze filament spool images and extract material type, brand, color, and specifications
- **Camera Integration**: Built-in camera capture functionality for scanning filament spools
- **Smart Label Generation**: Automatically creates formatted labels with QR codes for easy identification
- **Phomemo Printer Support**: Direct integration with Phomemo thermal printers for instant label printing
- **Batch Processing**: Generate multiple labels efficiently with batch operations
- **Template Gallery**: Pre-designed label templates for different use cases
- **Analytics Dashboard**: Track filament usage and inventory statistics
- **Material Library**: Maintain a database of scanned filaments for future reference

## Target Users
- **3D Printing Enthusiasts**: Hobbyists managing multiple filament spools who need organized inventory tracking
- **Maker Spaces**: Shared workshops requiring systematic filament organization and user accountability
- **Small Manufacturing**: Businesses using 3D printing that need professional inventory management
- **Educational Institutions**: Schools and universities with 3D printing labs requiring material tracking

## Use Cases
- Quickly identify filament properties without manual data entry
- Generate professional labels for filament storage organization
- Track filament usage and remaining quantities
- Create QR codes for digital inventory management
- Maintain consistent labeling across multiple printer setups
- Streamline workflow in multi-user environments
